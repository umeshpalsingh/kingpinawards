About Us

About King Pin Awards

King Pin Awards is a dedicated online supplier specializing in high-quality bowling awards, gifts, and memorabilia. Designed to serve bowling leagues, tournament organizers, and individual enthusiasts, the company provides a comprehensive catalog of products to celebrate every "strike" and milestone on the lanes.

Our Product Range

We take pride in our extensive selection, which includes:

Custom Awards

Bowling trophies, plaques, and medals.

Unique Memorabilia

Miniature wooden bowling pins and balls.

Apparel & Accessories

Custom bowling towels (including clearance and sale items), shoe tags, and lapel pins.

Novelty Gifts

Key chains, mouse pads, magnets, jewelry, and dry-erase boards.

League Essentials

Patches and pin displays for merit achievements.

Why Choose Us?
  • Convenience: Our entire inventory is available for secure online purchase.
  • Accessibility: For those who prefer traditional methods, we offer printed catalogs and the ability to mail or fax orders using downloadable forms.
  • Customer Support: We provide dedicated support via phone and email to ensure every order meets our customers' specific needs.
